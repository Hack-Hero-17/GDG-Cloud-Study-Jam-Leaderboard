About GenAI Study Jam

The GenAI Study Jam is an immersive experience that dives deep into Generative AI technologies. Explore the concepts, applications, and potential of GenAI, learn about the latest tools, and participate in hands-on sessions to gain practical knowledge. Whether you're a beginner or experienced in AI, this program is designed to elevate your understanding and skills in Generative AI.
